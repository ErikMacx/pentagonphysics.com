E₈ Cross-Reference — McKay Correspondence
The 9 eigenspaces of the 600-cell adjacency matrix correspond to the 9 irreducible representations of the binary icosahedral group 2I, which form the 9 nodes of the extended E₈ Dynkin diagram. Multiplicities sum to |2I| = 120 (one full 600-cell). Galois-invariant (rational) eigenvalues total 94 modes; Galois-active (irrational, √5-involving) eigenvalues total 26 modes, paired under √5 → −√5.

Physical reading
• The 94 invariant modes are the confined sector — Galois-fixed, coupling only via α⁰ (strong) through the D₄ bridge.
• The 26 active modes are the free sector — Galois-paired, coupling via α at the Galois boundary (electromagnetic).
• Weak interactions use both channels (α² × σ²/2); gravity cascades through the 18 occupied confined eigenmodes (α¹&sup8; × 12/7).
• Via McKay, each of the 9 irreps is a node of 𝘦₈ (extended E₈ Dynkin diagram) with Coxeter label = irrep dim.
