Why Gravity Couples to Mass

DOI
10.5281/zenodo.19109094
Read full paper on Zenodo →

Gravity couples to all forms of mass-energy regardless of charge, colour, or any other quantum number. This universality is a postulate in general relativity (the equivalence principle). Pentagon Physics proves it. Gravity arises from the trivial representation of the binary icosahedral group 2I — the representation in which every group element acts as the identity. A mode in the trivial representation couples to everything equally, by definition. Universality is not a postulate. It is a consequence of gravity arising from the representation where all symmetry operations are trivial.

\[ \rho^\text{trivial}(g) = 1 \;\forall\, g \in 2I \quad \Rightarrow \quad \text{universal coupling} \]
